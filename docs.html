Meet HAL

HAL is our voice-activated robotic assistant who recognises a few basic commands, including:

Pleasantries

  • “OK HAL”
  • “Goodbye HAL”
  • “Thankyou HAL”
  • “Show me the help”

Basic maths

  • “What is 2 + 3?”
  • “What is 5 - 3?”
  • “What is 5.3 * 2.4?”
  • “What is 15 / 2?”

Reading the News

  • “Read me the news”
  • “How many stories?”
  • “How many (national) stories?”
  • “Read me the (national) news”

Basic robotics

  • “What are the three laws?”
  • “Open the pod bay doors, HAL”

This project is an exploration of the Web Speech API, which allows voice recognition (speech to text), as well as speech synthesis (text to speech). More information can be found on the Google Developers site.
